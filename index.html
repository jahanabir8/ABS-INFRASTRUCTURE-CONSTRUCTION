WELCOME TO ABS INFRASTRUCTURE & CONSTRUCTION

ABS Construction was founded in 2014 and since then continued rising with reputation while growing diligently undertaking all the operations. Over the last 8 years, because of the company’s firm commitment to a high standard of infrastructural performance, the company reached a position where we take pride today.

ABS Construction is capable of performing a big range of infrastructure activities of civil engineering, prefabricated steelworks, architecture, tower & telecommunication and related supportive engineering activities with the help of highly qualified technical and skilled personnel.

ABS Construction has successfully installed optical fibers and towers for local owners and companies and enlisted with many local big companies in Bangladesh as well

Piling (Onshore & Offshore)

We work closely with our clients and ensure the best design and most cost-effective technologies are used to complete each project. We have adequate equipment specifically designed to work in and around existing structures. We can put piles practically anywhere which is a cost-effective alternative for clients who are interested in developing outside of the urban city.

Road construction

To provide people with a safe transportation and mobility experience roads must be long-lasting and secure for driving to avoid endangering users and the environment. While doing the construction of roads, it is mandatory to prevent repair expenses as much as possible.

Steel structure

We develop a range of structure projects starting from residential to the factory within a favorable budget. It is either our office or residence apartment, where we spend the majority of our time a day and as a development company, we understand your safety concerns. That is why we recommend steel structures for most of our projects when we find it necessary.

Architectural service

With architectural design service, we give a construction project a valid layout with exact measurement and shapes that is almost close to reality. The layout is an integral part of the architecture that is essential for carrying out a construction development project. You can get a clear conceptual vision of your project prior to its development through our 3D animation on architecture concept design.

Structure and superstructure design

We prepare the design of the structure and superstructure based on the following process. Initially, we collect general information and review the architectural design for building framing. We start with program formulation where we decide the building will be built, storied numbers, basement Floors, units on each floor, foundation soil, types of exposures, surrounding structures, floor to floor Height, use of rooftop and ground floor, etc.

Building electrical and telecommunication tower construction services

As a part of our construction and development work, we provide electrical design services to help plan the design of the structure. During designing plans, our team assists building designers in preparing integrated electrical systems. We have completed several projects on tower construction for different renowned telecommunication companies, like Teletalk, Grameenphone, Robi Axiata, Nokia solution, etc.
