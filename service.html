PILLING (Onshore & Offshore)

We work closely with our clients and ensure the best design and most cost-effective technologies are used to complete each project. We have adequate equipment specifically designed to work in and around existing structures. We can put piles practically anywhere which is a cost-effective alternative for clients who are interested in developing outside of the urban city. With the help of skilled and expert workers, we make sure to do everything safely and efficiently while piling. Since pilling creates noise for surrounding we take every precaution to minimize disruptions to nearby people and inhabitants. We do bored piling using a hydraulic drilling apparatus, by creating a vertical hole drilled into the soil. We have a thorough understanding of bored pile construction and engineering, as well as soil and site conditions that a highly specialized operation demands. By impact hammering, vibrating, or pushing it into the ground to a design depth, a driven precast pile is installed. Specifically, when we find the soil in an aggressive condition, we conduct this kind of piling though it is allowed to be performed in all kinds of construction work. Since this process is faster in terms of installation, we conduct it frequently if required. The groundwater cannot affect while driven precast pile installation takes place.

With the help of a team of experienced engineers and workers, who are experienced with driven precast pile installation, we offer the standard piling solution at your service.

While doing the construction of roads, it is mandatory to prevent repair expenses as much as possible. It is difficult to build and maintain sturdy and lasting roads since they are exposed to extreme circumstances. However, we the keep cautious about the quality of components in road products, particularly bitumen since it varies greatly. Mainly better quality of bitumen is responsible for the extreme strength of the road to be exposed by heavy vehicles. Our service of road construction shows the standard of road materials and how they react to the heavy loads and exposed deterioration. We have the knowledge and experience necessary to take care of such types of projects. In terms of testing and evaluating the performance and durability of road materials and products, our expertise and proper experience save many mismanagements.

STRUCTURE AND SUPERSTRUCTURE DESIGN

We prepare the design of the structure and superstructure based on the following process. Initially, we collect general information and review the architectural design for building framing. We start with program formulation where we decide the building will be built, storied numbers, basement Floors, units on each floor, foundation soil, types of exposures, surrounding structures, floor to floor Height, use of rooftop and ground floor, etc. The following stage framing system is finalized based on the architectural design and there are different types such as load-bearing, beam, column, tubular, and bundle tube systems, etc. A primary foundation type gets selected after analyzing the soil test report. Mainly from two types of foundations - footing foundation and pile foundation, we decide which one is applicable based on the sub-soil bearing capacity. A very integral part of structural design is load calculation, where we calculate the bearing capacity of the building or structure. After conducting load calculation we conduct structural analysis that delivers data for the next stage which is structural design. Finally, the process ends with delivering the printed sheet of the design to clients.
