HIGH VOLTAGE TRANSMISSION LINE

Our Company experienced in the field of High Voltage Transmission Line construction (voltage ranges from 132kV to 400kV) as a turnkey contractor. We work under BPDB, PGCB, DPDC, Summit Power & some other organizations as Turnkey Contractor. We also work as specialized sub-contractor in the same field under foreign organization like LTB Leitungsbau GmbH Germany, KEC International Ltd. India, Angelique International Ltd. India, L&T India. The main scopes of work in our organization, these fields are as follows;- Route survey, Profile drawing, tower spotting using PLS-Cadd,

  • Sub-soil investigation
  • Design & supply of line materials including installations
  • Design of tower foundations including Pile design – Installation of tower foundation including piling job
  • Erection of tower members, stringing of phase conductor including OPGW & EW
  • Testing & Commissioning

We have full setup of experienced engineering team & construction team for High Voltage Transmission Line job and have full setup of equipment for the same. We have one set TSE equipment (Tensioner 9ton and Puller 7ton) to pull twin conductor simultaneously.
