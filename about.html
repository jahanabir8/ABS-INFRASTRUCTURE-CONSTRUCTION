ABS Construction believes in contributing to the life of people and serving with utmost positive reasons. With long years of experience in constructing structures, pilling, architecture, road construction, building electrical set up and telecommunication tower infrastructural project management, we have completed projects for Teletalk 3G project, ASB BTS & MW link project, 3G project for Banglalink by Nokia, ENA-DONGA Rajuk apartment projects and cell addition sites of Grameenphone Roll-out project.
